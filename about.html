This is me.

It all started when I was born...
Okay... I won't go that far back. I just want to tell you a little bit about myself.
My name's Austin Conner and I grew up near Kingwood, West Virginia.
Ever since I could remember, I was the families IT Guy. If someones computer was messed up, I was the guy to talk to.
I was constantly researching things. YouTube was my happy place. I loved finding out different ways to use technology.
I found my way to a Minecraft Sever Hosting website and that's where my curiouosity florished.
From dark to sunrise, late into the night, I was trying to make my virtual world better. I had more fun managing the server than actually playing the game.
One day, my friends stopped playing and the fun went away. The server was shut down and never turned on again.
After I graduated high school, I didn't really know what I wanted to do with my life. I didn't feel like I was good at anything.
A few months after graduating, I got a job working with the Geek Squad in Morgantown. Working at Best Buy put the nail in the coffin.
I need to be doing something in tech. Fast foward to now, I'm pursuing a Computer Infomation Systems Bachelors degree from WVU Potomac State College.
I'm hoping I'll gain the knowledge to allow me to see the world through a different lens. One day, I want a job that offers me a challange. I want to do something important. I want to create. I want my work to affect the world. Hopefully one day I'll achieve that, but for now...
it's just a dream...
